# Pass 46 — Accessibility and Human Factors

Pass 46 hardens Invisible Air for real human use without changing any scientific state, source, geometry, interpretation, or claim boundary.

## Changes
- Added a keyboard-visible skip link and stable main-content targets.
- Added strong `:focus-visible` treatment across links, buttons, summaries, selects, and interactive elements.
- Raised minimum text sizes and contrast for the ten-second guide, legend, Gatehouse support text, Atlas scientific states, temporal trace, captions, and navigation.
- Enlarged interactive targets toward a 44-pixel minimum.
- Improved Gatehouse select, compact control, scrolling, and narrow-screen full-height behavior.
- Added robust reduced-motion behavior and high-contrast / forced-colors support.
- Improved 125–200% zoom resilience through fluid typography and responsive stacking.
- Preserved color-independent labels: Reported, Measured, Contextual, Inferred, Source, Field, Time, Trust, and Judgment remain written as text.

## Scientific boundary
Accessibility changes presentation only. They do not alter evidence, atmospheric interpretation, terrain analysis, uncertainty, or claim authority.
