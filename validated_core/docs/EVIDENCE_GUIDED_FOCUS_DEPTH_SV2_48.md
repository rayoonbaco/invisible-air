# SV2-48 — Evidence-Guided Focus and Depth Composition

This pass adds restrained depth composition to separate measured terrain, visual atmospheric reconstruction, confidence, and uncertainty without converting visual emphasis into scientific evidence.

## Guardrails
- No simulated optical depth of field is used as evidence.
- Blur and glow never encode concentration or plume thickness.
- Uncertainty remains visible and may receive increased emphasis.
- Reviewer interaction always takes priority.

## Boundary
Focus, blur, glow, contrast, and depth cues guide attention only. They do not measure distance, concentration, plume thickness, hidden geometry, or atmospheric structure.
