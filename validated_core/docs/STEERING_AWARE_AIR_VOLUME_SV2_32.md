# SV2-35 — Steering-Aware Air Volume

This pass uses the validated terrain steering field and its confidence surface to apply bounded visual modulation to the layered air volume. Curvature, compression, lateral bias, asymmetry, and uncertainty expansion are confidence-gated.

The resulting scene is a visual review heuristic only. It is not CFD, plume transport, concentration, plume height, mass, exact geometry, or proof of actual airflow.
