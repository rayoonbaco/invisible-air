# SV2-35 — Terrain-Responsive Particle Advection

SV2-35 lets the visual tracer particles respond gradually to confidence-gated terrain steering evidence. Motion remains subordinate to the broad wind pathway and uses bounded along-path envelopes for alignment, channeling, shelter, opposition, lateral deflection, and downstream uncertainty.

## Scientific boundary

Particles are visual tracers, not measured methane parcels. Their motion does not represent concentration, mass, exposure, travel time, CFD, or verified atmospheric transport.
