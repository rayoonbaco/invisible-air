# SV2-53 — Reviewer-Guided Evidence Exploration

SV2-53 introduces an evidence explorer that lets a reviewer inspect one scientific family or evidence thread at a time without deleting, concealing, or overstating the underlying science.

## Capabilities

- Evidence-family filtering
- Search across review threads
- Contextual inspector cards
- Evidence relationship links
- Breadcrumb and return-to-overview control
- Muting of unrelated layers rather than removal
- Direct access to deep review routes
- Persistent uncertainty and claim-boundary access

## Scientific boundary

Evidence exploration changes reading order and visual emphasis only. It does not add evidence, increase confidence, detect methane, reconstruct transport, quantify emissions, or attribute responsibility.
