# SV2-38 — Convergence and Terrain-Focused Accumulation Potential

This pass derives restrained map-registered convergence and focused-accumulation potentials from validated terrain, steering confidence, and available current atmospheric context.

The convergence layer identifies locations where DEM-derived downslope directions appear to focus. The focused-accumulation layer combines convergence with lower terrain, flatness, and available basin support.

Neither layer is measured airflow, methane concentration, pollutant trapping, exposure, CFD, or proof of accumulation.
