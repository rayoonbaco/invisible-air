# SV2-50 — Evidence-State Color, Contrast, and Visual Hierarchy Governance

SV2-50 coordinates color, contrast, saturation, opacity, and reading order across the scientific scene.

It gives uncertainty a protected visibility floor, limits simultaneous accents, keeps direct evidence distinct from modeled and inferred layers, and prevents color intensity from acting like concentration, probability, severity, or plume thickness.

The pass is a visual-governance layer only. It adds no scientific evidence and does not alter plume geometry, particle transport, air-volume physics, or source attribution.
