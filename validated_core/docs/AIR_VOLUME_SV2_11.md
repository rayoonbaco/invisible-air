# SV2-11 — Air Volume

This pass changes the plume from a mostly particle-led trace into a layered visual body of air.

## Visual layers
- diffuse outer haze
- continuous middle body
- restrained luminous core
- three small vertical offsets that create depth without claiming measured height
- particles retained as motion cues rather than the whole plume

## Evidence boundary
The layer is a map-registered visual reconstruction for human review. Opacity, apparent density, blur, size, and vertical separation do not encode measured methane concentration, plume height, mass, emissions rate, or exact source-product geometry.
