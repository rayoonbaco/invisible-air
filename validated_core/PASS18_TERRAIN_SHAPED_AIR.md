# Pass 18 - Terrain-Shaped Air

## Purpose
Move deliberately into Level 2 of the Invisible Air strategy: the viewer should be able to see that terrain changes the possible atmospheric movement without opening a scientific panel.

## Changes
- Replaced the uniform earned breath with five coherent stream families.
- Channeling and convergence visibly narrow the field.
- Lee response and divergence visibly broaden the field.
- Ridge response adds restrained lift through the existing registered geometry.
- Uncertainty breaks continuity and lowers downstream coherence.
- Widened the opening camera slightly so source, terrain response, and uncertainty remain in one frame.

## Scientific boundary
This remains a human-review reconstruction using measured terrain and available atmospheric context. It is not detected methane, verified transport, concentration, exposure, or attribution.

## Acceptance test
With labels ignored, a first-time viewer should see the field narrow, bend, broaden, and fragment in relationship to the landscape.
