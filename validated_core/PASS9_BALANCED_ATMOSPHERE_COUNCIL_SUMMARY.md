# Pass 9 — Balanced Atmosphere Council

## Specialist council
- Atmospheric scientist: preserve evidence limits; avoid concentration or detected-plume semantics.
- Geospatial visualizer: make terrain legible as a shaping actor, not a decorative basemap.
- Uncertainty specialist: encode weakening support through fragmentation, widening, fading, and discontinuity.
- Motion designer: create a continuous source-to-field story with slow, coherent movement.
- Interface reduction editor: keep Essence nearly silent; defer all explanation.
- Skeptical human reviewer: require a 12-year-old to understand source, movement, and fading confidence in ten seconds.
- Implementation engineer: preserve Context, Science, citations, audit routes, and all established tests.

## Consensus strategy
The scene must tell one visual sentence: a restrained source begins a possible air path; terrain shapes the motion; the field broadens and breaks as evidence weakens. Ambient air supports atmosphere but never competes with the evidence path. Particles remain only as nearly invisible orientation texture.
