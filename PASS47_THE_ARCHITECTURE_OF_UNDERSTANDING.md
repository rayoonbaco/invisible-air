# Pass 47 — The Architecture of Understanding

Invisible Air now assigns one clear responsibility to each room.

- Observatory — orient through the phenomenon.
- Gatehouse — interpret the active observation.
- Atlas — document and compare cases.
- Library — trace and verify evidence.
- Chapel — judge what the evidence permits and withholds.
- Workshop / About — explain the principles holding the instrument together.

This pass changes information architecture, labels, and handoffs only. It does not change scientific inputs, source geometry, wind context, terrain interpretation, uncertainty, or claim authority.
