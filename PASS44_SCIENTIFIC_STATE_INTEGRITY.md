# Pass 44 — Scientific State Integrity

Invisible Air now distinguishes four scientific states across the Gatehouse and Atlas:

- **Reported** — a traceable source or observation record says something occurred or was observed.
- **Measured** — a sensor, survey, or validated dataset directly supplies a property.
- **Contextual** — later or surrounding information helps interpretation but does not recreate the observation.
- **Inferred** — a review hypothesis derived from other inputs; never a direct observation.

## Critical correction

The phrase **100% evidence package** has been removed from the public interface. It is replaced with:

> **Case record complete · Interpretation unresolved**

Record completeness now describes administrative field coverage only. It cannot be read as scientific certainty, verified transport, concentration, exposure, attribution, or responsibility.

## Ventura–Oxnard state ledger

- Reported: traceable source record.
- Measured: validated elevation and terrain data.
- Contextual: current wind, explicitly not observation-time wind.
- Inferred: possible movement reconstruction for review.

Reserved cases remain incomplete and interpretation is withheld.
