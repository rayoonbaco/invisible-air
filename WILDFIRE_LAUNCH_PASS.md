# Wildfire Launch Pass

## Objective
Make the Atmospheric Observatory immediately relevant to wildfire-smoke interest without representing the demonstration as a live forecast.

## Changes
- `/observatory` now opens the Four Corners scenario with the fine-smoke aerosol profile.
- The opening language changes when smoke is active.
- Added an explicit demonstration-only boundary.
- Increased local terrain visibility while preserving the dark observatory atmosphere.
- Tightened the right-side reading load.
- Added a restrained smoke-specific visual identity.
- Terrain comparison links now preserve the selected material.

## Scientific boundary
No governing physics, field normalization, scenario coordinates, or live-atmosphere calculations were changed. The smoke surface remains a relative modeled influence demonstration, not a wildfire forecast, AQI measurement, exposure estimate, or health advisory.
