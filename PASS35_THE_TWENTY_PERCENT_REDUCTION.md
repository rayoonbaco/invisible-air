# Pass 35 — The Twenty-Percent Reduction

## Purpose
Remove visible interface weight without removing scientific capability, evidence boundaries, provenance, or human-review responsibility.

## Changes
- The Atlas status strip was removed; evidence readiness remains attached to each observation.
- Land, Air, Time, Trust, and the human question now live behind a quiet disclosure instead of competing with the Field Insight.
- The Observatory Gatehouse now leads with the selected observation and its single scientific sentence; comparison mechanics remain one click away.
- The Chapel lost its redundant introduction and uses fewer separators and less panel weight.
- Library shelves became open reading surfaces instead of boxed software cards.
- The About room was reduced to one premise and three verbs: Observe, Question, Review.
- Shadows, borders, fills, labels, and secondary prose were reduced across the Campus.

## Protected
All routes, evidence fields, uncertainty language, provenance, scientific records, human review, and the validated core remain intact.
