# Pass 37 — Scientific Personality

Invisible Air now speaks with one disciplined scientific temperament: **The patient observer**.

## What changed

- The first-minute narration now follows a consistent cadence: notice, qualify, withhold, invite review.
- Live terrain, wind, and missing-evidence states shape the language without expanding the claim.
- The instrument speaks specifically and calmly rather than sounding like a generic AI assistant.
- A quiet disclosure explains the voice and makes its boundary explicit.
- The Chapel and annotation sequence retain their human-review authority.

## Scientific boundary

Scientific personality changes cadence, never claim authority. It does not fabricate observations, promote modeled behavior into measurement, or infer transport, concentration, exposure, responsibility, or illegality.
