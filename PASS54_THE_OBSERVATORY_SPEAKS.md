# Pass 54 — The Observatory Speaks

## Objective
Complete the drag interaction with one restrained, deterministic scientific observation describing how the modeled display changed.

## What changed
- After a source is moved and recalculated, the Observatory compares the previous and new independent fields.
- One controlled sentence appears after the field transition settles.
- The sentence may describe displayed overlap, modeled corridor direction, crosswind spread, supported footprint, or a broadly similar pattern.
- The observation is derived from governing-model diagnostics and geographic field bounds; it is not generated prose.
- The result is announced through an accessible `aria-live` status region.
- Undo and Reset clear the prior observation.

## Scientific boundary
The sentence describes change inside the relative-influence model. It does not claim measured smoke concentration, exposure, AQI, fire behavior, chemical interaction, or forecast certainty.

## Scope deliberately excluded
No chatbot, history log, report panel, additional tab, freeform AI text, or new model claim was added.
