# PASS 60 — The Experiment Journal

## Purpose
Preserve the chain of experiments without turning the Observatory into a dashboard.

## Added
- Session-persistent journal of source moves and additions.
- Deterministic What changed / Why entries.
- Source coordinates, material, and atmospheric context.
- Reopen Experiment restores its source configuration and recomputes fields.
- Export Notes downloads a plain-text laboratory record.

## Boundary
The journal preserves modeled experiments. It is not a record of measured releases, verified incidents, exposure, or concentration.
