# Pass 39 — Science Becomes Visible

The Atlas now translates scientific state into visible form before explanation.

## Added
- A case-specific phenomenon sketch for every Atlas observation.
- Ventura shows source, possible directional field, rising terrain, and a dashed uncertainty gap.
- Reserved cases remain ghosted and explicitly withheld rather than receiving fabricated scenes.
- Land, Air, Time, and Trust now carry compact visual marks tied to case state.
- Missing time and withheld trust are represented as gaps, not completed graphics.

## Boundary
These marks summarize the evidence state already present in the case package. They are not new measurements, transport models, concentration estimates, exposure findings, or attribution.
