# Pass 38 — The Living Atlas

The Observation Atlas now behaves as a scientific memory rather than a static case list.

## What became alive

- Each case carries an honest state: **Observation awake** or **Waiting for evidence**.
- A three-part evidence sequence shows what has been retained, what context is current, and what remains unresolved or withheld.
- The Atlas remembers what presently holds, what could change the interpretation, and why the current conclusion must not be promoted.
- Reserved cases remain visibly dormant. They do not animate into false evidence or generate atmospheric scenes.

## Scientific boundary

The Living Atlas records changes in evidence state, not merely the passage of time. Motion communicates attention, not new measurement. No clock, pulse, or visual effect implies that fresh evidence has arrived.

## Intended experience

The Atlas should feel like a field notebook that can wake when evidence changes: quiet, cumulative, and honest about absences.
