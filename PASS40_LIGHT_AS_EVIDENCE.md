# Pass 40 — Light as Evidence

Light now behaves as a scientific reading aid rather than decoration.

- Warm light anchors the reported source.
- Cooler, weaker illumination follows the evidence-bounded possible field.
- Relief receives restrained directional light where terrain changes plausibility.
- The light thins before unsupported certainty, preserving the visible evidence gap.
- Illumination unfolds with the first-minute observation and never claims new measurement.

## Gatehouse correction

The expanded Gatehouse now occupies the right-side reading bay on desktop instead of obscuring the source and plume. Its typography, contrast, spacing, and scroll behavior were strengthened for ordinary PC viewing and reading-glasses accessibility.

## Boundary

Light encodes the current evidence state. It is not methane detection, concentration, exposure, verified transport, or attribution.
